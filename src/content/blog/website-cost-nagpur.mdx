## What Determines the Cost of a Website?

Website pricing is not arbitrary. Every rupee in a quote reflects real decisions about time, technology, and expertise. Here are the primary factors:

### 1. Number of Pages and Complexity

A 5-page brochure site is fundamentally different from a 50-page corporate portal with a booking system. More pages mean more content to design, develop, and test. Complexity — custom calculators, user logins, payment gateways — adds significant development hours.

### 2. Design Approach: Template vs. Custom

Many low-cost providers use pre-built WordPress themes or website builders like Wix. These are fast and cheap, but they come with limitations in performance, uniqueness, and SEO. Custom designs built from scratch cost more upfront but deliver better results and a stronger brand impression.

### 3. Functionality and Integrations

Basic contact forms are simple. But eCommerce, appointment booking, CRM integration, live chat, multi-language support, and custom admin dashboards each add to the scope. These features require skilled development time.

### 4. Content Creation

Many business owners underestimate the cost and effort of content. Professional copywriting, photography, and video production are often not included in developer quotes. Budget for these separately or ask your agency if they offer content services.

### 5. SEO and Performance Optimization

A website that nobody can find is a wasted investment. On-page SEO setup, structured data, Core Web Vitals optimization, and Google Analytics configuration should be part of any professional build — but not all agencies include these by default.

## Typical Website Costs in Nagpur (2026)

Here is a realistic breakdown based on current market rates from reputable agencies in Nagpur:

### Basic Business Website: ₹15,000 – ₹30,000

This covers a 4–6 page responsive website on a WordPress template or simple custom theme. Suitable for small businesses, freelancers, and local shops that need a basic online presence. Includes a contact form, mobile responsiveness, and basic SEO.

**What you get:** Professional online presence, contact form, mobile-friendly design.
**What you don't get:** CMS for self-editing, advanced SEO, speed optimization.

### Professional Business Website: ₹40,000 – ₹80,000

This is the sweet spot for most Nagpur businesses. A 10–20 page custom or semi-custom WordPress site with a CMS (so you can update content yourself), on-page SEO, blog functionality, Google Analytics, and 3–6 months of support.

**Who it's for:** Healthcare clinics, law firms, real estate businesses, educational institutions, restaurants, and service businesses.

### eCommerce Website: ₹60,000 – ₹1,50,000

An online store on WooCommerce or Shopify includes product catalog management, shopping cart, payment gateway integration (Razorpay, PayU, or Stripe), order management, and basic SEO. Complexity and number of products significantly affect cost.

### Custom Web Application: ₹1,00,000 – ₹5,00,000+

School ERPs, hospital portals, booking systems, B2B platforms, and multi-user dashboards fall into this category. These are engineered from scratch using React, Node.js, or similar modern technologies. Cost reflects development hours, not templates.

## Why Cheap Websites Cost More in the Long Run

A ₹5,000 website sounds appealing. But consider what you are actually buying:

**Slow loading speeds.** Cheaply built sites on shared hosting with unoptimized code frequently score below 50 on Google PageSpeed Insights. Slow websites lose visitors — Google reports that 53% of mobile users abandon a page that takes more than 3 seconds to load.

**Poor SEO foundations.** If your website was built without proper heading structure, metadata, schema markup, or mobile optimization, it will struggle to rank on Google regardless of how much you spend on SEO later.

**Security vulnerabilities.** Outdated themes, nulled plugins, and unsupported code are common in cheap builds. A single malware attack can result in your site being blacklisted by Google — a recovery that can take weeks and cost far more than a proper build would have.

**No CMS.** Many low-cost developers hand over a static site with no admin panel. Want to update your phone number or add a new service? You are calling the developer and paying for every change.

**No ownership.** Some developers host your site on their own shared account, meaning if you stop paying them or they close their business, your website disappears.

## Questions to Ask Any Nagpur Web Design Company

Before signing a contract, ask these questions:

- Will I own the domain, hosting, and website files outright?
- Is the design custom or template-based? Can I see similar work?
- Is on-page SEO included? What specifically?
- Will I be able to edit the website myself after launch?
- What is included in post-launch support and for how long?
- Have you built websites for businesses similar to mine?
